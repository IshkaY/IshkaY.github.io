How to play?

Make it to the end of the cave and collect the Diamond.

Avoid dying to Enemies or running out of oxygen.

Collect Pickups to restore oxygen.

Kill enemies to make progression



Game Controls

W - Moves Player Forward

A = Moves Player Left

S - Moves Player Backwords

D - Moves Player Right

Left Click - Shoot Bullets at enemies

P - Pause Game

Enter - Restart/Play Game
